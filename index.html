About Us


We are an international trading company that continues to strive to increase its market share both upstream and downstream to encourage the creation of a strong and globally competitive logistics trading business ecosystem.
With the integration of services between trade, logistics and digitalization, end-to-end services are created with a single point of contact.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and reputable world-class national trading company.

Our Mission

Carry out general and special trading for food and non-food products from upstream to downstream, targeting domestic and international markets;

Providing quality products, competitive prices, and benefits to ensure customer satisfaction, and establish sustainable partnerships;

Designing an integrated ecosystem that functions to support the main business in trading;

Increasing the productivity, agility and quality of HR performance through the development of employee capabilities and competencies.

Chicken

Chicken is the most common type of poultry in the world. It can be prepared in a vast range of ways, including baking, grilling, barbecuing, frying, and boiling.

Duck

Duck is used in a variety of dishes around the world, most of which involve roasting for at least part of the cooking process to aid in crisping the skin. Some dishes use parts of the duck as an ingredient along with other ingredients.

Quail

Quail is a food that is commonly consumed. There are quite a lot of fans, because the taste of quail meat is delicious and not much different from chicken meat.
